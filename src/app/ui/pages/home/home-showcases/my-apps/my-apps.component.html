Ethical Implications of AI in Financial Decision-Making This study explores the ethical challenges of integrating AI into financial systems, focusing on issues like algorithmic bias, transparency, accountability, and public trust. It proposes a comprehensive ethical framework with clear guidelines, governance, and regular audits to ensure responsible AI use. The goal is to balance innovation with fairness and integrity, supporting informed decision-making and ethical best practices across the financial sector.
